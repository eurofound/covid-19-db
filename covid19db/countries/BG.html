Overview of measures by country

Bulgaria

Eurofound has identified 27 measures in Bulgaria in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Supporting businesses to stay afloat with 8 cases (30%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, with the next main update scheduled for July 2020.

This overview was prepared by Tsetomila Sabcheva and Luboslav Kostov (Citub) and Eurofound.
