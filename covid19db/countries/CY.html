Overview of measures by country

Cyprus

Eurofound has identified 14 measures in Cyprus in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Income protection beyond short-time work with 4 cases (29%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, with the next main update scheduled for July 2020.

This overview was prepared by Loucas Antoniou (INEK) and Eurofound.
