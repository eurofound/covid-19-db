Overview of measures by country

Belgium

Eurofound has identified 79 measures in Belgium in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Supporting businesses to stay afloat with 22 cases (28%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Dries Van Herreweghe (Katholieke Universiteit Leuven) and Eurofound.
