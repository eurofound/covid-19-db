Overview of measures by country

Luxembourg

Eurofound has identified 80 measures in Luxembourg in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Promoting the economic, labour market and social recovery into a green future with 27 cases (34%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Patrick Thill (LISER) and Eurofound.

Measures to lessen the impact of the inflation and energy crisis on citizens

Governments across the EU continue to implement policies to support citizens and businesses in the face of rising food and energy prices caused by the COVID-19 crisis and intensified by the war in Ukraine.
