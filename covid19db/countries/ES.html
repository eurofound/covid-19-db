      COVID-19: Policy responses across Europe

Overview of measures by country

Spain

Eurofound has identified 88 measures in Spain in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Promoting the economic, labour market and social recovery with 25 cases (28%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, with the next main update scheduled for July 2020.

This overview was prepared by Oscar Molina (UAB) and Eurofound.
