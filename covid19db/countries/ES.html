Overview of measures by country

Spain

Eurofound has identified 110 measures in Spain in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Promoting the economic, labour market and social recovery with 26 cases (24%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Oscar Molina (UAB) and Eurofound.
