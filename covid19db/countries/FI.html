Overview of measures by country

Finland

Eurofound has identified 19 measures in Finland in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Employment protection and retention with 6 cases (32%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, with updates added on an ongoing basis.

This overview was prepared by Amanda Kinnunen, Oxford Research, and Eurofound.
