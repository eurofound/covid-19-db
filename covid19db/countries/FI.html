Overview of measures by country

Finland

Eurofound has identified 74 measures in Finland in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Supporting businesses to stay afloat with 15 cases (20%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Amanda Kinnunen (Oxford Research) and Eurofound.

30 January 2023

 

Measures to lessen the impact of the inflation and energy crisis on citizens

Governments across the EU continue to implement policies to support citizens and businesses in the face of rising food and energy prices caused by the COVID-19 crisis and intensified by the war in Ukraine. This article summarises the policy responses as reported in Eurofound's EU PolicyWatch database from January to September 2022.

First responses to cushion the impact of inflation on citizens
