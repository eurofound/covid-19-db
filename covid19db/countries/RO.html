Overview of measures by country

Romania

Eurofound has identified 62 measures in Romania in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Supporting businesses to stay afloat with 19 cases (31%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Victoria Stoiciu (European Institute of Romania) and Eurofound.
