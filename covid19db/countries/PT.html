Overview of measures by country

Portugal

Eurofound has identified 114 measures in Portugal in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Promoting the economic, labour market and social recovery with 26 cases (23%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Heloísa Perista, Maria da Paz Campos Lima, Paula Carrilho, Ana Brázia (CESIS) and Eurofound.

Measures to lessen the impact of the inflation and energy crisis on citizens

Governments across the EU continue to implement policies to support citizens and businesses in the face of rising food and energy prices caused by the COVID-19 crisis and intensified by the war in Ukraine. This article summarises the policy responses as reported in Eurofound's EU PolicyWatch database from January to September 2022.
