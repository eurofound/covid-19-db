Overview of measures by country

Malta

Eurofound has identified 52 measures in Malta in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Promoting the economic, labour market and social recovery with 13 cases (25%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Luke Anthony Fiorini (University of Malta) and Eurofound.
