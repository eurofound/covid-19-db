Overview of measures by country

Sweden

Eurofound has identified 13 measures in Sweden in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Income protection beyond short-time work with 4 cases (31%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, with the next main update scheduled for July 2020.

This overview was prepared by Anna-Karin Gustafsson, Oxford Research, and Eurofound.
