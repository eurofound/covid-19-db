Overview of measures by country

Germany

Eurofound has identified 92 measures in Germany in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Supporting businesses to stay afloat with 22 cases (24%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Birgit Kraemer (Hans Boeckler Foundation) and Eurofound.
