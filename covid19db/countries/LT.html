Overview of measures by country

Lithuania

Eurofound has identified 61 measures in Lithuania in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Supporting businesses to stay afloat with 17 cases (28%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Inga Blaziene (Lithuanian Social Research Centre) and Eurofound.
