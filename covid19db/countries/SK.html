Overview of measures by country

Slovakia

Eurofound has identified 58 measures in Slovakia in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the war in Ukraine, the digital transformation, the green transition, and companies undergoing restructuring. Most cases fall into the category Employment protection and retention with 12 cases (21%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated at regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Rastislav Bednarik, Ludovit Cziria, Daniela Keselova, Miroslava Kordosova (IVPR) and Eurofound.
