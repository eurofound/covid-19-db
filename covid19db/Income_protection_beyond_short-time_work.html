Overview of measures by category

Income protection beyond short-time work

What formal or legal measures are in place in your country to support workers who have been put on to short-time working by employers or who have been given temporary leave with reduced rates of pay, unpaid leave, or who have been made redundant (i.e. who still formally have jobs but who are either not working or working reduced hours)? Has the coverage of the social benefit system which provides income support to workers who lose their jobs been widened in the present crisis in order to ensure that those concerned are protected?
