Background information

The Ministry of Labour issued the Circular 14676/253/2020 dated 7 April 2020, according to which businesses-employers will be able to make corrections/additions or revocations of their original declaration forms, in order to benefit from the financial support measures related to the category of companies - employers whose business activity was suspended by order of a public authority or businesses that have been financially affected as per their Activity Code Numbers (ACN).

Content of measure

Businesses that have been financially affected as per their Activity Code Numbers (ACN), or their operation has been suspended under a state decision and the employees of such businesses are allowed to make corrections / additions or revocations of their original declaration forms, within the specified time intervals, in order to benefit from the financial support measures. Beneficiaries include: for employers that are affected based on their ACN: From 10 to14 April and 21 to 23 April 2020. For employers that are closed under a state decision: From 10 to 13 April 2020; For employees: From 1 to 13 April, 21 to 24 April and 1 to 3 May 2020.
