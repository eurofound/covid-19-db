Overview of measures by category

Protection of workers at the workplace

Cases in this category contain examples of emergency legislation, agreements or company practices concerning occupational safety and health (OSH) and worker well-being, particularly for workplaces with significant client contact where social distancing is difficult, such as the care sector, retail or banks. It also includes new work arrangements aimed at limiting the direct face-to-face contacts, in particular through teleworking, but also via new working time arrangements.

Overall, Eurofound has identified 32 measures in 16 countries for this category.
