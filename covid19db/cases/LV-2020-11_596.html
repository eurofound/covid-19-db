Background information

A special regime for the submission of annual reports is introduced through Section 15 of the law “On Measures for the Prevention and Suppression of Threat to the State and Its Consequences Due to the Spread of COVID-19” (adopted on 20 March 2020, valid from 22 March 2020). According to this measure, the Minister for Finance is empowered to extend the terms for the preparation and submission of the tax statements specified in Sections 30, 31, and 32 of the Law on Budget and Financial Management and in the regulations issued on the basis thereof, and also the terms for the provision of opinions on statements.

Content of measure

The new measure, in detail, establishes that: - an undertaking (company) to which the Law on the Annual Financial Statements and Consolidated Financial Statements applies is entitled to submit the annual statement and the consolidated annual statement (if applicable) on 2019 within a term which exceeds the ordinary one by three months; - an association or foundation is entitled to submit the annual statement on 2019 or part thereof to the State Revenue Service by 31 July 2020; - a religious organisation is entitled to submit the annual statement on 2019 or part thereof to the State Revenue Service by 31 July 2020.
