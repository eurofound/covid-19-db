Background information

Taking into account the specific nature of this economic sector, linked to its fragility and its dependence on context and seasonality, the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté Region will create an emergency fund which, in addition to the measures already put in place by the government, the public bank BPI France and the Region, will make it possible to provide additional aid to meet their urgent cash needs and help safeguard them.

Content of measure

The region must submit to the Regional Council for a vote on April 24 the creation of: - a €2.5 million fund to help event companies; - a €2.5 million Tourist Accommodation Support Fund, also worth €2.5 million. Tourism This aid fund for tourist accommodation is aimed exclusively at hotels, campsites, residential leisure parks, villages and holiday centres, group lodgings with a minimum of 16 beds (establishments receiving the public) in a single unit. Other sectors of activity (catering, leisure, bed and breakfast and tourist accommodation with fewer than 16 beds, etc.) are not eligible. - This flat-rate aid will amount to €5,000 per undertaking. For information, the application period should be from 1 to 31 May 2020 (deadline for submission of the application).
