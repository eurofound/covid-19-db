Overview of measures by category

Measures to prevent social hardship

Cases in this category contain measures aimed at supporting those in urgent need because of the pandemic and deteriorating economic situation or international crises. It gathers policy initiatives aimed at protecting vulnerable groups of peoples (beyond employment support), preventing over-indebtedness, securing 'a safe home' by restricting evictions or via deferrals of rent and mortgage payments, providing humanitarian aid, as well as examples of measures to provide services in kind to the most vulnerable.

Overall, Eurofound has identified 236 measures in 30 countries for this category.
