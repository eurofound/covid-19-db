Overview of measures by category

Measures to prevent social hardship

Cases in this category contain measures aimed at supporting those in urgent need, because of the pandemic and deteriorating economic situation. It gathers policy initiatives aimed at protecting vulnerable groups of peoples (beyond employment support), preventing over-indebtedness, securing 'a safe home' via restricting evictions or deferrals of rents and mortgage payments, as well as examples of measures to provide services in kind to the most vulnerable.

Overall, Eurofound has identified 40 measures in 17 countries for this category.
