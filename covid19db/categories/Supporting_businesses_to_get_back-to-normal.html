Overview of measures by category

Supporting businesses to get back-to-normal

Cases in this category include any measure aimed at supporting businesses to get back to normal, after the immediate emergency situation and beyond. This includes cases related to the gradual relaunch of businesses as well as various forms of business support to generate revenue and maintain and (re-)create jobs.

Overall, Eurofound has identified 10 measures in 6 countries for this category.
