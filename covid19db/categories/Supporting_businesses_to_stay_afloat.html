Overview of measures by category

Supporting businesses to stay afloat

Cases in this category mainly include arrangements to support companies to stay afloat financially. This may include support for those companies that had to shut down their activities due to the public health measures in the first place, but ultimately also those which are indirectly affected. The types of measures in this category relate to access to finance (including bank guarantees or easing access to credit), direct subsidies for companies (excluding those related to wages) as well as regulations concerning the deferral of payments (for instance of tax or social security obligations), as well as changes to rescue procedures in case of insolvency or the adaptation of insolvency regulation.

Overall, Eurofound has identified 50 measures in 13 countries for this category.
