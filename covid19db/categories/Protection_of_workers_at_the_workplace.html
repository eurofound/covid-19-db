Overview of measures by category

Protection of workers at the workplace

Cases in this category contain examples of emergency legislation, agreements or company practices concerning occupational health and safety (OSH) and worker well-being, particularly for workplaces with a higher number of client contacts, such as in retail or banks or with patient contacts. It also includes new work arrangements aimed at limiting the direct face-to-face contacts, in particular on teleworking, but also via new working time arrangements.

Overall, Eurofound has identified 27 measures in 14 countries for this category.
