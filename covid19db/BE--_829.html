Background information

TExt copied from the Croatian presidency document: For workers who will be working entirely at home for some time because of COVID-19 employers can grant an office allowance up to €126,94 per month which covers heating, electricity, small office equipment, ... This can be granted without social security contributions to all workers who work at home, even to workers who did not work at home before the Covid-19 measures and for whom the employer had not formally concluded a teleworking agreement.
