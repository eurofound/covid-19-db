Overview

The EU PolicyWatch database currently contains 2,213 measures introduced by governments and social partners to cushion the social and economic effects on businesses, workers and citizens. The data collection started in 2020 and was last updated in March 2023. More information is available on the Eurofound website:

  • About EU PolicyWatch
  • Topic: COVID-19
  • Topic: Stand with Ukraine

Use the side navigation to find measures by country, type of measure, category, or target group. Use the dashboard to search and filter policy measures on the following topics by more than 15 attributes:

  • COVID-19: 1660 policy measures
  • War in Ukraine: 357 policy measures
  • Green Transition: 119 policy measures
  • Digital Transformation: 74 policy measures
  • Restructuring Support Instruments: 298 policy measures

Target groups

The database indicates for each measure to which target groups it applies, broadly differentiating between workers, businesses and citizens.

The database contains 42 specific target groups, including for example:

  • Workers: Self-employed, Seasonal workers, Platform workers
  • Businesses: SMEs, Start-ups, Larger corporations
  • Citizens: Parents, Older citizens, Migrants
