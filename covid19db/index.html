Overview

EU PolicyWatch currently contains 1769 measures introduced by governments and social partners to cushion the social and economic effects on businesses, workers and citizens. The data collection started in 2020 and was last updated in October 2022. More information is available on the Eurofound website:

  • About EU PolicyWatch
  • Topic: COVID-19
  • Topic: Stand with Ukraine

Target groups

The database indicates for each measure to which target groups it applies, broadly differentiating between workers, businesses and citizens.

The database contains 42 specific target groups, including for example:

  • Workers: Self-employed, Seasonal workers, Platform workers
  • Businesses: SMEs, Start-ups, Larger corporations
  • Citizens: Parents, Older citizens, Migrants
