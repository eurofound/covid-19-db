      COVID-19: Policy responses across Europe

Overview

COVID-19 EU PolicyWatch currently contains 945 measures introduced by governments and social partners to cushion the social and economic effects on businesses, workers and citizens.

  • Topic: COVID-19
  • Data: COVID-19 EU PolicyWatch

Use the side navigation to find measures by country, type of measure, category, or target group.

Categories

Target groups

The database indicates for each measure to which target groups it applies, broadly differentiating between workers, businesses and citizens.

The database contains 42 specific target groups, including for example:

  • Workers: Self-employed, Seasonal workers, Platform workers
  • Businesses: SMEs, Start-ups, Larger corporations
  • Citizens: Parents, Older citizens, Migrants
