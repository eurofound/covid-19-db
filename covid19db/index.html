      COVID-19: Policy responses across Europe

Overview

COVID-19 EU PolicyWatch currently contains 1354 measures introduced by governments and social partners to cushion the social and economic effects on businesses, workers and citizens.

  • Topic: COVID-19
  • Data: COVID-19 EU PolicyWatch

Use the side navigation to find measures by country, type of measure, category, or target group.

Categories

Target groups

The database indicates for each measure to which target groups it applies, broadly differentiating between workers, businesses and citizens.

The database contains 42 specific target groups, including for example:

  • Workers: Self-employed, Seasonal workers, Platform workers
  • Businesses: SMEs, Start-ups, Larger corporations
  • Citizens: Parents, Older citizens, Migrants

Role of employers' organisations in designing the measure

  • In 235 measures, there was no involvement;
  • For the 165 remaining measures the involvement is unknown, or they were considered as being out of the social partner's domain.

Role of trade unions in designing the measure

  • In 425 measures, trade unions were involved:
  • In 252 measures, there was no involvement;
  • For the 184 remaining measures the involvement is unknown, or they were considered as being out of the social partner's domain.

  • In the remaining 717 measures, there was no involvement or the involvement is unknown.
